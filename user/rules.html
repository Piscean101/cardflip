HOW TO PLAY
After you press PLAY and select a game mode, you will be brought to the board page (shown below)
Once you're ready, press the START button

Then click on any of the cards that appear to reveal them
BE PREPARED: Once the timer starts, if you don't beat the level, it will count as a loss
Try to match as many face-down cards (top) to the text in the glowing panel (middle)

You can use clues (left) to help you find all the matches you need (right) to win the game!
TIP: Don't waste your flips, they are limited. Be careful not to let the timer run out!
Select the PROFILE tab on the Home screen to review your play history

Keep playing to earn more EXP, level up your skills and unlock new game modes!
